## Overview

<Note>
The RWAX Protocol is built to integrate Real World Assets (RWAs) into a user-friendly ecosystem for retail users, with USDR being the key stablecoin that:
</Note>

- **Maintains Value Stability**: USDR is pegged to real-world assets, ensuring its value remains stable against the USD, offering a buffer against the volatility of cryptocurrencies.

- **Facilitates RWA Engagement**: USDR enables users to interact with tokenized RWAs directly, providing a stable means of exchange within the ecosystem.

- **Enhances Access**: By using a stablecoin backed by tangible assets, RWAX makes interacting with RWAs more accessible, promoting financial inclusivity.
